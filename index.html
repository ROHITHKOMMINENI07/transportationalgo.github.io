Course Introduction

What is the course about?

The Design and Analysis of Algorithms course helps us understand how to design and evaluate algorithms to tackle problems effectively. We explore key concepts like time and space complexities, as well as various data structures and algorithms.

Real-Time Applications

How do we connect the course with real-time applications?

Algorithms are everywhere in our daily lives. Search engines use graph algorithms to show relevant results, and online shopping platforms use sorting and searching algorithms to suggest products. Transportation and logistics companies use algorithms to optimize routes and reduce costs. Financial institutions rely on algorithms for risk assessment and fraud detection.
